our story

Founded in Montréal in 2025, the Institut de Recherche et Exploration Musicale (IREM) was born at the crossroads of music, engineering, and fabrication. The idea had been building for years, born from the frustration of watching great creative and technical problems go unsolved because no one was willing to work across both worlds at once. In late 2024, a grant from the Conseil des arts et des lettres du Québec (CALQ) gave the team the validation and momentum to make it real.

Today, IREM operates as a Canadian engineering and R&D organization, delivering software platforms, 3D-fabricated prototypes, and acoustic research to clients and institutional partners. Our philosophy remains unchanged: if it doesn't work as well as it sounds, it doesn't ship.
